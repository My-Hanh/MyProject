ABOUT



Every small craft begins with an inspiration that leads to an idea. With a pencil in hand and a sheet of paper in front of you is all you need to create a small work of art.

Cute, happy, little things in everyday life give us joy in life. These little joys are captured as posters, postcards and stickers to share with others. Happiness and love simply have to be shared. <3

Enjoy the little works of art and let yourself be infected by love, joy and happiness.
